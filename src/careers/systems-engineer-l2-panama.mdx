## Required Clearance and Education:

- Top Secret//SCI with Full Scope Poly
- Technical Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related discipline + fourteen (14) years of related experience as a SWE
- Five (5) years of additional SE experience may be substituted for a Bachelor's degree
- Must have experience with:
  - At least 14 years troubleshooting software installations and operations
  - Understanding of computer networks with ability to identify, troubleshoot, and document network connectivity and performance issues
  - Tier 1 (Help Desk) and Tier 2 (Escalation) problem identification, diagnosis, and resolution of problems
  - At least 5 years analyzing and correcting data flow issues within systems
  - Experience in understanding network diagrams and schematics to troubleshoot dataflow and computer system related issues
  - Working knowledge of enterprise dataflow and Nifi
  - **Must be willing to perform Panama shift work. This team works 12hr shifts, they rotate every 8 weeks, and have every other weekend off.**

Good to have:
- Comfortable interacting with customer, users and other teams regarding system information and troubleshooting

## Job Details:

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Systems Engineer Level 2 to join our dynamic team as a Systems Engineer/Systems Administrator to implement, troubleshoot and maintain Information Technology (IT) systems. The System Engineer will:

- Configure, install, manage and troubleshoot UNIX and Windows operating systems and GOTS/COTS software
- Support to IT systems including day-to-day operations, monitoring and problem resolution for all of the client/server/storage/network devices
- Optimizes system operations and resource utilization, and performs system capacity analysis

- Develop simple data queries for existing or proposed databases or data repositories
