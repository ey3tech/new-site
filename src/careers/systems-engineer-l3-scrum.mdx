## Job Details:

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Systems Engineer Level 3 to lead the overall schedule and development of a mission dashboard. The SE will dissect high-level requirements into user stories and manage incremental development rhythms through testing and development.

This role is integral to supporting a small, dedicated group of analysts in their operational efforts. The ideal candidate will bring extensive experience in infrastructure, data centers, and cloud computing services to our team. As a technical leader, you will play a critical role in integrating requirements, design, and technology, ensuring seamless operation and efficiency of our systems.
